Product core

Designed for discipline that feels sustainable.

Traditional habit tools overemphasize dashboards and streak pressure. Track.now keeps the user close to action through mission-based progress, momentum scoring, grace-friendly feedback, and a quieter visual system.

🎯

Mission durations

Turn habits into 21, 45, or 90-day arcs so users feel progress and completion, not infinite maintenance.

Momentum over shame

Identity feedback
